Film from the Bayerische Staatsoper's 2013 production of Lucia di Lammermoor - 8mm film, Andergrand Media + Spektakle

Andergrand Media + Spektakle, based in Warsaw, Poland, has been designing video and lighting design for theatrical productions around Europe since 2010. Its clients include the Bavarian State Opera, Teatr Polski in Wroclaw, Schauspielhaus Vienna, Kammerspiele in Munich, Warsaw National Opera, Teatr Polski Bydgoszcz. The company's philosophy is to focus on ideas, not technology, to create the images that best serve the organizing concept of the production. It was founded by theatre director and designer Michal Zadara, and works with collaborators based all around the world - from New York, Kuala Lumpur, to Warsaw and Vienna.
